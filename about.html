About essellay

We turn AWS Service-Level Agreements into real dollars—automatically.
AWS promises up to 100 % service credits when their uptime slips, yet most teams leave that money unclaimed because the process requires manual data collection, complex calculations, and strict deadlines. essellay.ai (pronounced "S-L-A") removes the friction:

  1. Select your AWS services. We show you exactly which CloudWatch metrics to export for each of our six supported services.
  2. Upload the CSV files. Our browser-based engine processes CloudWatch exports and calculates availability against official SLA thresholds.
  3. Get your claim ready. We generate the AWS Support request with calculated percentages and credit amounts, plus create a ZIP file with all evidence.

Why we built this

Every AWS SLA includes the same requirement:

"Open a Support case within two billing cycles and include timestamps and logs."

Extracting metrics from CloudWatch, calculating error rates, and mapping to credit tiers takes hours. We automate these calculations so you can focus on building instead of parsing spreadsheets.
