About essellay

We turn AWS Service-Level Agreements into real dollars—automatically.
AWS promises up to 100 % service credits when their uptime slips, yet most teams leave that money unclaimed because the process is fiddly and time-boxed. essellay.ai (pronounced "S-L-A") removes the friction:

  1. Choose your AWS services. We guide you to the exact CloudWatch Metrics you need—no guesswork.
  2. Drag-and-drop the CSVs. Our LLM-powered engine measures availability, error rates, and latency against the official SLA thresholds.
  3. Copy your ready-made claim. We generate the Support Center request with timestamps, percentages, and your account ID. Paste, attach the CSV, hit Submit—done.

Why we built it

Every AWS SLA says the same thing:

"Open a Support case within two billing cycles … include timestamps and logs."

Collecting that evidence by hand is tedious. We automate the math so you can focus on shipping, not sifting through spreadsheets.
